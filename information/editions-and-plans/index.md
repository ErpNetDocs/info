# @@name Editions

## Overview

The **@@name platform** is a comprehensive business management system intended for **all companies**. 
Its purpose is to enable all businesses to enjoy the benefits of **ERP**, **CRM**, and **Team Collaboration** software, unconstrained by their size, industry, location, structure, or business model.  

Delivered as **web-based**, cloud-hosted software, the platform is ready to support your business ventures any time, any place, on any device - in the office, at home, or on the go.  

The @@name platform is designed to cater to the changing needs of the diversity of industries, as well as to be **flexible and adaptive** to all companies, regardless of trade, size, or other factors.  

For this, the @@name platform is offered in **several editions**, available through **subscription plans**. 
Each edition comes with a **specific purpose** and is geared towards the needs of various organizations and industry sectors, providing the solution they would best **benefit from**.  

All editions of **@@name** are intended to:

* help business users build a strong foundation for their teamwork
* streamline and enjoy smooth business processes and company operations
* boost productivity through reducing manual and repetitive actions and
* maximize the profit they get in return for their efforts and investments.  

This way **@@name** empowers companies to overcome the traditional and modern challenges in every industry, as well as the unique ones their business faces.

### [@@name Express](express-edition.md)

**@@name Express** is a scalable and universal solution that is fast and easy to **implement and set up** by your company.  

The purpose of @@name Express is to streamline and accelerate universal business processes, such as communication, event planning and scheduling, task management, file sharing, contact management, information retrieval, marketing and sales, project management, and various other business activities.  

The **Express** edition comes with integrated:  

* Personal productivity app suite
* Team collaboration module
* CRM system for marketing and sales process management
* Project management app suite
* Business app suite

all unified in a single platform and accessible via the same user interface.  

### [@@name Small Business](small-business-edition.md)

**@@name Small Business** edition provides a fully functional, **complete ERP-CRM** pltaform intended to bring the benefits of both ERP and CRM systems to **all small to medium enterprises** (SMEs).  

**Small Business** unifies a comprehensive set of modules and apps for supporting all your business operations and strategic decision making:

* The Personal productivity and team collaboration from Express
* The CRM system from Express
* Project management app suite from Express
* Product management and pricing
* Logistics and supply chain management (SCM)
* Manufacturing and assembly processes
* Order management
* Invoicing and Payments
* Accounting and tax reporting
* Financial management and analysis
* and many other integrated modules and functionalities

### [@@name Enterprise](enterprise-edition.md)

**@@name Enterprise** is the **corporate-level edition** of the @@name platform designed to run in **large business structures** like corporations, holdings, multinational enterprises, and others alike.  

Enterprise edition enables your corporation to utilize a **large-scale business management platform** with all modules and apps included throughout **one or multiple owned** companies and subsidiaries.  

Created to maintain and support highly sophisticated business logic, multi-stage complex workflows, and highly intensive data processing and integrations, Enterprise ensures **quick and easy scaling** of the processing power and computational capacity necessary for your business to operate with cost effectiveness, highly optimized performance, and maximum productivity.  

> [!TIP]
> **The @@name platform** is designed as an all-around ERP-CRM platform with **built-in modules, apps, and features**.  
>
> These are **standardized**, **connected to each other in real time**, and **fully integrated within the platform itself**.  
> 
> This enables your business to have the edition of your choice **directly implemented from scratch**, as well as make a **smooth transition** from one edition to the next, **retaining all of your data, settings, and configurations for your system**.
