    ERP.net Editions

    Overview

    The ERP.net platform is a comprehensive business management system intended for all companies. Its purpose is to enable all businesses to enjoy the benefits of ERP, CRM, and Team Collaboration software, unconstrained by their size, industry, location, structure, or business model.

    Delivered as web-based, cloud-hosted software, the platform is ready to support your business ventures any time, any place, on any device - in the office, at home, or on the go.

    The ERP.net platform is designed to cater to the changing needs of the diversity of industries, as well as to be flexible and adaptive to all companies, regardless of trade, size, or other factors.

    For this, the ERP.net platform is offered in several editions, available through subscription plans. Each edition comes with a specific purpose and is geared towards the needs of various organizations and industry sectors, providing the solution they would best benefit from.

    All editions of ERP.net are intended to:

    • help business users build a strong foundation for their teamwork
    • streamline and enjoy smooth business processes and company operations
    • boost productivity through reducing manual and repetitive actions and
    • maximize the profit they get in return for their efforts and investments.

    This way ERP.net empowers companies to overcome the traditional and modern challenges in every industry, as well as the unique ones their business faces.

    ERP.net Editions Description

    ERP.net platform is available in several editions:

    ERP.net Express

    ERP.net Express is a scalable and universal solution that is fast and easy to implement and set up by your company.

    The purpose of ERP.net Express is to streamline and accelerate universal business processes, such as communication, event planning and scheduling, task management, file sharing, contact management, information retrieval, marketing and sales, project management, and various other business activities.

    The Express edition comes with integrated:

    • Personal productivity app suite
    • Team collaboration module
    • CRM system for marketing and sales process management
    • Project management app suite
    • Business app suite

    all unified in a single platform and accessible via the same user interface.

    ERP.net Small Business

    ERP.net Small Business edition provides a fully functional, complete ERP-CRM pltaform intended to bring the benefits of both ERP and CRM systems to all small to medium enterprises (SMEs).

    Small Business unifies a comprehensive set of modules and apps for supporting all your business operations and strategic decision making:

    • The Personal productivity and team collaboration from Express
    • The CRM system from Express
    • Project management app suite from Express
    • Product management and pricing
    • Logistics and supply chain management (SCM)
    • Manufacturing and assembly processes
    • Order management
    • Invoicing and Payments
    • Accounting and tax reporting
    • Financial management and analysis
    • and many other integrated modules and functionalities

    ERP.net Enterprise

    ERP.net Enterprise is the corporate-level edition of the ERP.net platform designed to run in large business structures like corporations, holdings, multinational enterprises, and others alike.

    Enterprise edition enables your corporation to utilize a large-scale business management platform with all modules and apps included throughout one or multiple owned companies and subsidiaries.

    Moreover, the Enterprise edition provides a solid foundation for integrations with external web-based, desktop, and mobile systems and apps that are specifically designed for the individual needs of your business.

    ERP.net Enterprise is an excellent choice for your corporation or multi-national business as it is created to support:

    • Highly sophisticated business logic
    • Multi-stage complex workflows
    • Highly intensive data processing
    • High number of integrations and simultaneous external app processing and connections
    • Solution cost effectiveness based on scale
    • Highly optimized performance thanks to various automations
    • Quick and easy scaling of the processing power and computational capacity
    Tip

    The ERP.net platform is designed as an all-around ERP-CRM platform with built-in modules, apps, and features.

    These are standardized, connected to each other in real time, and fully integrated within the platform itself.

    This enables your business to have the edition of your choice directly implemented from scratch, as well as make a smooth transition from one edition to the next, retaining all of your data, settings, and configurations for your system.
